A little bit about me

​I am a certified ACE personal trainer and continuing my education in nutrition and performance enhancement. I am married with two CRAZY boys and one behemoth sized dog. I am an animal lover is an understatement. I haven’t had the story book beginnings into heath and fitness. I was never an athlete or into fitness until much later in my life. I was the nerdy shy wallflower. Admittedly, I have had my own struggles. I have spent years validating myself worth through others approval never feeling good enough no matter what efforts I made. I have been broken down, isolated, and made to feel less than. I had completely lost who I was. I felt stuck and afraid of change, of the unknown. It wasn't until I took time out to take care of ME when things started to change. I slowly began finding my strength and self-worth through fitness. Through my own personal passage, I have found my passion in helping others. I love working with individuals who are ready to tap into their potential. I am a safe space for those who have lost their way and individuals who are ready for a change. No matter if your goal is weight loss, muscle gain, balance or more education. I’m ready to help you achieve some RESULTS all while keeping you motivated and having a good laugh!
